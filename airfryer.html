Description

Make mealtimes easier, healthier, and more delicious with our sleek 9L Stainless Steel Digital Air Fryer. Designed with families in mind, its generous single basket capacity is perfect for cooking larger portions — ideal for everyday dinners or entertaining guests.

Equipped with an easy-to-read digital LED display and soft-touch controls, it's never been simpler to select the perfect cooking settings. Choose from 10 one-touch preset menus including fries, wings, pizza, and roasts for quick, consistent results at the tap of a button.

The adjustable temperature range from 80°C to 200°C and 60-minute timer let you fine-tune your cooking for a wide variety of dishes. The smart pause function adds extra convenience — pause cooking by removing the basket and resume right where you left off once it's returned.

With a non-stick coated basket and dishwasher-safe parts, cleaning up is just as easy as cooking. Stylish, practical, and family-ready, this air fryer brings versatility and modern convenience to your kitchen.
