The Instant Vortex incorporates Instant Pot's famous smart program technology to deliver the best results every time. The stylish design takes up minimal countertop space and the intelligent touchscreen and dial controls makes it an absolute pleasure to use.

4-in-1

Air Fry, Roast, Bake and Reheat. Use the pre-set Smart Programs to air fry juicy chicken wings, crispy fries, roast cauliflower bites, bake calzones or mini pizzas, and quickly reheat last night's dinner — all without losing any flavour or crunch!

EvenCrisp Technology

The latest generation of air frying technology ensures the fastest cooking times and most delicious results. Replaces hot oil with super-heated circulating air to produce the same crunchy taste without the added grease.
